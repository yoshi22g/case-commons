Experts in Child Welfare, Older Youth, Juvenile Justice, and Equity Delivering Results for Human Services

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing technical assistance, education, training, research, and thought leadership on behalf of public and private agencies focused on reforming human services in child welfare, juvenile justice, and mental health across the country with a particular emphasis on data and technology.

What We Do

Systems and Operations Innovation for agencies working in:

Child Welfare: We help leadership and staff better understand the complexity of their systems and how to transform policy, practice, and finance and operations to improve outcomes for kids and families.

Older Youth: We provide technical assistance to public and private agencies working in partnership with each other and young people to improve how communities and states address the needs of young people transitioning from foster care to adulthood.

Juvenile Justice: We work with Juvenile Justice leaders and staff, young people, families, and communities to achieve positive outcomes for court-involved youth and to promote community safety.

Equity: We work with public and private system leaders and staff to advance and embed race equity and inclusion across all levels of decision making, policies, and practices designed to improve outcomes for all children, youth, and family.
