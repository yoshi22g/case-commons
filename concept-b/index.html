Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing technical assistance, education, training, research, and thought leadership on behalf of public and private agencies focused on reforming human services in child welfare, juvenile justice, and mental health across the country with a particular emphasis on data and technology.

What We Do

Systems and Operations Innovation for agencies working in:

Child Welfare: We help leadership and staff better understand the complexity of their systems and how to transform policy, practice, and finance and operations to improve outcomes for kids and families.

Older Youth: We provide technical assistance to public and private agencies working to improve how communities and states address the needs of young people transitioning from foster care to adulthood.

Juvenile Justice: We work with Juvenile Justice leaders and staff, young people, families, and communities to achieve positive outcomes for court-involved youth and to promote community safety.

Equity: We work with public and private system leaders and staff to advance and embed race equity and inclusion across all levels of decision making, policies, and practices designed to improve outcomes for all children, youth, and family.

Positive Impact on Children and Families

The foundation of our work includes a passionate commitment to improve the health and well-being of the children and families who have become involved in these well known, but poorly regarded (OR distrusted and ineffective) systems. For us that means

  • More equitable outcomes and less racial and ethnic disparities
  • More permanence and less disconnection from lifetime supports
  • More children and youth living in homes and less in institutions
  • More stability and fewer disruptions in placements and schools
  • More prevention & community supports for families and less system involvement
  • More healing and less trauma
  • More stable & quality workforce and less turnover

Our Expertise

We are dedicated to accelerating change across human service systems to improve outcomes for the country’s most disadvantaged children, youth, and families

  • Advanced Technical Assistance and Training provided by seasoned system reform experts who work in partnership with local teams
  • Systems Analysis in-depth qualitative and quantitative analysis to determine key leverage points for improvements
  • Innovation and Best Practices tools and models to enhance existing work
  • Data Analysis and Predictive Analytics interpretation and utilization of data in decision-making throughout the agency
  • Worker Satisfaction and Retention tools to recruit, train, and retain employees in human services
  • Equity and Inclusion tools to assess and measure improvements across service delivery
  • Trauma-Informed, Positive Youth Development, Promising Practices tools for the most effective methods of service delivery in human services
  • Policy and Practice Reform informed by the youth and families impacted
